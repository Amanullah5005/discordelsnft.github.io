Roadmap

January, 2023

"The traits of our Discordels NFT collection will be shuffled for the first time, and all the doodling Discordels will be released from NFT generators. We will then unveil the first sneak peeks of the collection. Don't worry, these are just the initial traits - more will be added on a regular basis as the project progresses."

Febraury, 2023

"In February 2023, we have some exciting plans in store! We'll be running some huge collaborations and focusing on building the most fun and positive NFT community ever. We're also planning to expand our presence on social media and host mega giveaways for our Discordlist and Free Mint spots. Stay tuned!"

March, 2023

"As we near the launch of our Discordels NFT collection, we will conduct a poll in our server to determine the preferred marketplace for the launch. Options may include Clutchy or Bluemove and more, and the selected marketplace will serve as the launchpad for the collection. This will allow us to gauge the preferences of our community and ensure that the launch is tailored to meet their needs."

April, 2023

"In April, we will start collecting whitelist addresses for our Discordels NFT collection. We will gather the addresses of OGs, Legends, and Promembers, as well as all other roles separately. This will allow us to carefully manage the distribution of the collection and ensure that it is available to all members of our community.It will be also followed by testnet mints in march."

May, 2023

"If the Sui mainnet launches before or in May, we will finalize the launch of our Discordels NFT collection. To celebrate the occasion, we will offer special discounts on the collection for holders of higher roles in the server. Additionally, we will select a few lucky members of the server to receive free copies of the collection. Information about the free minters will be shared in server announcements, so be sure to keep an eye out for updates."
